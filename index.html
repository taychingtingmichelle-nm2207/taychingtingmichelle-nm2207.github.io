In this day and age, social media has become almost a necessity for us. Furthermore, technology has advanced so rapidly that we even carry social media within our smart phones no matter where we are on the go. By definition, social media refers to the means of interactions among people in which they create, share, and/or exchange information and ideas in virtual communities and networks. Social media has become very essential in our daily lives for most of us.

This data story seeks to provide you with more knowledge regarding the usage, impact, pros and cons of social media.




Social Media Usage




Regional Social Media Users Worldwide (2022)

According to Statista, in 2022, Eastern Asia accounted for over 1.1 billion social media users, the majority of which were located in China. Additionally, Southern Asia had a total of 847 million social media users, whilst central Africa was home to just over 21 million users. And, if we generalise the data just by looking at the main 4 regions, namely Asia, Pacific, America, Europe, Africa, the proportion shows that Asia alone constitute 60.8% of all social media users worldwide. The generalised data can be found below as represented in pie charts and table form.

Average daily usage on Social Media worldwide (2022 Q2)

Based on the daily average screen-time (in 2022 Q2, May-Aug), TikTok champions every other platforms with a daily average screen time of 95 minutes. In a another survey done more recently in January 2023, results showed that FaceBook was in fact regarded the most popular social media platforms of all time. However, TikTok still manages to beat FaceBook in terms of screen-time. This also means that being the most popular social media platform does not guarantee a high average screen-time in comparison to others.





Growth of Instagram, Facebook, TikTok (2018 to 2022)

Comparing the growth of Instagram, Facebook and TikTok, we can see that Facebook still tops the charts based on the absolute number of users during this period. On the other hand, Instagram is still ahead of TikTok, having more annual users. Instagram and TikTok both are increasing at a steady and similar rate. However, the growth trend for Facebook can be seen to be going stagnant from 2021 onwards as the growth of users from 2021 to 2022 was merely 23 million.



Source: BusinessofApps



User Demographics of Instagram, Facebook and TikTok (By Age Group and Gender)

Now, let us look at the demographics of the social media platforms of TikTok, Facebook and Instagram. The following charts below presents the social media usage distribution by age group and by gender. These data are also based in 2022. The results tells us that TikTok users are mostly contributed by people aged 18-24, whereas Instagram users are mostly those who are aged 25-34. Similar to Instagram, Facebook also shows that most of its users are aged 25-34.

In addition, we can also see that the gender distribution for Instagram is almost identical as the only difference between the two gender percentage distribution is 1.6%. However, we can also observe that there are more male users than female users on Facebook and that there are more female users than male on TikTok.
